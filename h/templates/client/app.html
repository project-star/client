Log In to create Highlights, Annotations, Snips and Clips

How to get started

  1. To create a highlight, select text and click the  button on the popup.

  2. To create an annotation, select text and click the  button on the popup.

  3. To create a Video Snippet click the  button in the Sidebar margin. Add your notes to the Snippet and click Save. Wherever Video Snippets are supported the  button will turn Red

  4. To create an Audio Clip click the  button in the Sidebar margin. Add your notes to the Clip and click Save. Wherever Audio Clips are supported the  button will turn Red
